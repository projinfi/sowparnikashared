Capital County

Capital County villas - where peace, convenience, and value converge. Live grand and invest wisely in the lifestyle you deserve. Capital County is an aesthetically appealing villa project just a few kilometers away from Thiruvanthapuram railway station. It is conveniently located at Thrikkannapuram, Pappanamcode, one of the most happening spots in the capital, and also smartly priced. The project comprises 50 aesthetically designed villas, each exuding an aura of elegance and grandeur. Spacious interiors, coupled with modern amenities and high-quality finishes, create a haven of comfort and style. Sowparnika Capital County presents an exceptional opportunity to own a slice of luxury in the heart of Thiruvananthapuram. With its captivating design, spacious villas, unparalleled amenities, and ideal location, this project caters to those seeking a lifestyle that reflects their success and aspirations.

Variety of Options

Sowparnika Capital County offers a variety of villas to suit diverse needs and preferences. Choose from 3 BHK and 4 BHK configurations, ranging from 1303 sq. ft. to 2491 sq. ft. Each villa boasts well-ventilated spaces, natural light, and meticulously planned layouts that optimize space utilization.

Unparalleled Amenities

Residents of Sowparnika Capital County enjoy a plethora of amenities designed to elevate their lifestyle. From a serene swimming pool and a fully equipped gym to a vibrant children’s play area and a dedicated security system, the project caters to every aspect of your well-being.
