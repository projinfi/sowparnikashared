Why Choose a RERA-Certified Builder? Peace of Mind for Your Dream Home

Buying a home is a huge decision. It's an investment in your future and your family's well-being. That's why it's crucial to choose a builder you can trust. At Sowparnika, we're proud to be a RERA- certified builder, which means we're committed to transparency, accountability, and protecting your interests throughout the entire process.

What is RERA and Why Does it Matter?

RERA stands for Real Estate Regulation Authority. It's a game-changer in the Indian real estate industry, established to ensure fair practices and protect homebuyers. When you choose a RERA-certified builder like Sowparnika, you gain several key advantages:

Transparent Registration Process: RERA mandates that real estate projects must be registered prior to commencement, ensuring that all details, from project specifications to timelines, are disclosed upfront.

Streamlined Approval Process: With RERA, the approval or rejection of project registration is expedited within 30 days, providing clarity and efficiency in project initiation.

Accessible Project Information: RERA requires all project details, including approvals, timelines, and infrastructure provisions, to be readily available on their online portal, fostering transparency and ease of access for homebuyers.

Financial Accountability: RERA necessitates the segregation of funds through separate bank accounts for each project, ensuring that funds are utilized solely for the designated project and withdrawals are linked to project progress.

Timely Completion: Builders under RERA are obligated to adhere to project timelines, with penalties for delays, instilling confidence in homebuyers regarding timely delivery of their dream homes.
