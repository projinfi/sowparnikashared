Spacious Living in a Prime Location

Thycaud is a sought-after location for its promising social and physical infrastructure. Sowparnika Bhavani reflects this appeal, boasting spacious living areas within its single tower with 5 floors and 18 units. Spread over 0.21 acres, the project offers a sense of openness while remaining strategically located near essential amenities.

Investment Potential

Trivandrum’s real estate market is known for its stability and growth potential. Thycaud, in particular, is a hotspot for premium housing societies, making it an attractive location for both homeowners and investors seeking long-term value. The city’s flourishing job market further strengthens the demand for quality housing.
