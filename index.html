About Us

Delivering Happiness Since 2003

Sowparnika Projects stands as a prominent real estate developer in Bengaluru, a key hub within South India's thriving realty landscape. Our real estate endeavors span across the flourishing regions of Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu. Since our establishment in 2003, Sowparnika has become synonymous with delivering premium residential projects in the Bengaluru real estate market, all while ensuring affordability. With a proven track record, we have successfully completed over 50 projects and currently manage 25+ ongoing projects throughout Bengaluru and other pivotal cities in the region.
